2.    Welcome

Once a Rater is assigned, the administrator would issue a Login ID and password to the Rater. The Rater can then access the system with this ID. The Rater’s main role consists of providing feedback. After successful login, the Rater will be directed to the Welcome! page.

Figure 2. Main Page

3.    Rater’s To-Do-List

After clicking on Rater’s To-Do-List on the side bar, the Rater will be presented with a Rater’s To-Do List.  The Rater should provide feedback by clicking on each of the surveys, starting with the one that is at the top of the list.

For raters who are also Targets (Feedback Recipients), there will be a survey with Self under the heading Relation. Targets (Feedback Recipients) are expected to rate themselves because in the Individual Report there is a section on Blind Spots that compares the scores that the Target (Feedback Recipient) rated himself with the scores that he obtained from the others.

Figure 3. Rater’s To Do List

Some surveys may include preliminary questions like the ones below:

Figure 4. Preliminary Questions

The Rater should respond to each question by clicking on the rating selected. Use the scroll bar on the right hand side to scroll down to each question on the page. The Rater can move to the next page of questions by clicking on the > button. Clicking on the < button will return the Rater to the previous question and he/she can then change his entry when desired.

Figure 5. Sample Questionnaire

To see the definition of CPR or CP, simply mouse over the words CPR or CP to see the definition as indicated above. Certain surveys might only have CP without CPR.

If the Rater needs to log out before he completes his data entry, he can click on the Save button. Data entered will be saved and he will be returned to his last answered questions when he logs in again.

If the Rater clicks on the Finish button when there are questions which are unanswered, the system will display the following prompt:

Figure 6. Incomplete Questionnaire

For some surveys, additional questions as below may be included:

Figure 7. Additional Questions

Figure 8. Confirm Submission

Click on the OK button.

Figure 9. Final prompt

At this stage, the Rater would have completed providing feedback and can log out.

4.1         Change Password

The Rater can change his / her password by clicking on Tools, Change Password.

Figure 10. Change Password

4.2         Create Development Plan

This function is only available for Raters who are also Targets. Essentially this function allows the Target to create a development plan for those competencies that are identified as development areas based on the feedback from his / her Raters.

Click on the Add button to create a new Development Plan.

Figure 11. Individual Development Plan

Select the survey and the competency using the dropdown. Only competencies identified as development areas will be available on the list.  Select the Resource Type by clicking on the appropriate radio button and click on the Show My Plan button.

Figure 12. Individual Development Plan Selection


Select the Survey and Competency using the dropdown and the Resource Type using the radio button and click on Show. A list of all the relevant resources will appear.

Figure 13. Individual Development Plan Selection

Select the development activity or resource that you would like to include in your development plan. Use the calendar icon to fill in the Proposed Completion Date. You may select as many development activities or resources as you wish.

To view the completed Development Plan, click on Show My Plan.

Figure 14. Individual Development Plan List

To download a copy of the Development Plan into Microsoft Excel® format or to print a hardcopy of the Development Plan, click on the Print Development Plan button.

Figure 15. Individual Development Plan Excel
